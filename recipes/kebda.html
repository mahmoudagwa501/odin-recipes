Delicious Kebda

Ingredients:

  • Kebda Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Red Chilli

The Steps:

  1. Put the meet in the pan
  2. Put the pan on the stove
  3. cut the vegetables like ( green chilli , tomato , onion)
  4. Put the vegetables with the meet on the stove
  5. let them maybe 10 min and then
  6. The Meal is ready now
